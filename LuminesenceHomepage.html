Luminesence Project

What is the Luminesence Project?

The Luminesence Project is a website dedicated to
creating the oportunity to play video games on school computers.
The goal of the project is to create an oppertunity for people to
play video games when they are not normally allowed to.
Everybody should get the opportunity to do it.
